The Science

Botanical
by nature.

Cacao butter (Theobroma cacao seed butter) is extracted by cold-pressing cacao seeds — the same seeds that give us chocolate. It is one of the most stable natural fats on earth, rich in polyphenols, phytosterols, and a unique fatty acid profile that does something petroleum simply cannot: it penetrates the skin's lipid layer and actively nourishes from within.

While petroleum-based products sit on the surface as a temporary seal, cacao butter integrates with your skin's biology.

How It's Made

From cacao
tree to lips.

Every step of the cacao butter journey is traceable, intentional, and entirely free from industrial shortcuts. From harvest to lipstick — nothing is hidden.

01

Harvest

Cacao pods are harvested by hand from Theobroma cacao trees grown in Colombia's fertile lowlands. Each pod contains 20–50 seeds.

02

Ferment & Dry

Seeds are naturally fermented for 5–7 days — developing aroma and bioavailability of nutrients — then sun-dried to reduce moisture.

03

Cold-Pressed

Seeds are pressed at low temperature to extract pure cacao butter. No solvents. No refining. Every fatty acid and polyphenol preserved intact.

04

Blended & Set

Cacao butter is blended with our clean colour formula and set into the lipstick format — nothing else added.
