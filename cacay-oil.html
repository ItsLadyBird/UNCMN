The Science

Remarkable
by nature.

Cacay oil (from the Caryodendron orinocense tree) is the most nutrient-dense natural skin oil identified to date. Its unique fatty acid profile — dominated by linolenic acid — allows it to penetrate deeply without heaviness, delivering its extraordinary cargo exactly where the skin needs it most.

Dermatological studies consistently place it ahead of retinol serums, argan oil, rosehip oil, and vitamin C serums in independently measured skin renewal metrics.

How It's Made

From wild tree
to glass bottle.

Every step of the cacay oil journey is documented, verified, and shared with you. We call this full traceability — and it begins in the Colombian jungle.

01

Wild Harvest

Nuts are hand-harvested from wild cacay trees by local community pickers in the Colombian Amazon — no plantations, no monoculture.

02

Sun-Dried

Harvested nuts are naturally sun-dried for 7–10 days to reduce moisture and concentrate the oil content before pressing.

03

Cold-Pressed

Low-temperature pressing preserves every molecule of retinol, Vitamin E, and linolenic acid — nothing lost to heat, nothing added.

04

Unrefined

Zero refining, zero bleaching, zero deodorising. What reaches your hands is exactly what came from the tree — pure, intact, alive.
