Our Story

We are from
the land.

UNCMN was not built in a boardroom. It was built in the fields, forests, and communities of Colombia by people who understand the soil and everything that grows from it.

Manifesto

We believe that the most powerful ingredients grow wild, that the people who harvest them deserve fair recognition, and that the consumers who use them deserve the full truth about what they're putting on their skin.

We built UNCMN to close the gap between the soil and your hands — with nothing hidden, nothing added, and no one left behind.

Origin

Colombia,
at the source.

The cacay tree is native to Colombia's Orinoco basin and Amazon forest edge. Our farming and harvesting communities have worked these forests for generations — long before we came along. We joined their world; we didn't impose ours.

Every ingredient in our range carries GPS-level traceability. You can know the forest, the community, and the season it came from.

What We Stand For

Principles we
don't compromise.

01

Full Traceability

We document and share every link of our supply chain — soil coordinates, harvest dates, producer names. You have the right to know exactly what you are using and where it comes from.

02

Just Pricing

Our supply chain is lean by design. Fewer intermediaries mean more value for farmers at source and honest, fair prices for customers at the end. No margin extracted by those who add nothing.

03

Respect for All Beings

Human, animal, plant — every living being in our ecosystem is treated with dignity. We source seasonally, harvest sustainably, and never take more than the land can regenerate.

04

Community First

We are built by people from the land. The communities who harvest our ingredients are partners, not suppliers. Their knowledge, wellbeing, and futures are central to everything we do.

05

Zero Compromise on Purity

No synthetic additives. No fillers. No shortcuts. What we put in our products is exactly what we say is there — verified, traceable, and pure.

06

Ecosystem Stewardship

We exist to serve the ecosystem we came from. A percentage of every sale goes directly back into forest conservation and community development in the Colombian Amazon.

The UNCMN Promise

From soil to skin.

Every product we make carries this promise. It is the founding principle of UNCMN and it will never change — not as we grow, not under pressure, not for convenience.
